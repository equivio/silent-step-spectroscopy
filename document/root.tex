\begin{abstract}
\noindent
We provide an Isabelle/HOL formalization of Bisping and Jansen's weak spectroscopy game%
~\cite{bj2024silentStepSpectroscopyExpress}, which can be used to characterize a range of behavioral
equivalences simultaneously, spanning from stability-respecting branching bisimilarity to
weak trace equivalence.
We relate distinguishing sublanguages of Hennessy--Milner logic and attacker-winning budgets in an
energy game by an eight-dimensional measure of formula expressiveness.
\end{abstract}
